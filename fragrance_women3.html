  • JO MALONE LONDON

    Peony & Blush Suede Cologne

    RM560.00

    DESCRIPTION

    Fragrance Family: Floral
    Formulation: Spray

    What it is:
    A luxurious and seductive cologne - the essence of charm.

    What it does:
    Peonies in voluptuous bloom, exquisitely fragile, this cologne is flirtatious with the juicy bite of red apple and the opulence of jasmine, rose and gillyflower, mingled with the sensuality of soft, blush suede. Already love Peony & Blush Suede? Why not layer with another for a fragrance that’s you, with a twist. Peony & Blush Suede adds a floral opulence and luxurious sensuality – we love it paired with Blackberry & Bay for a fruity floral, deliciously juicy and temptingly fresh or Wood Sage & Sea Salt for an opulent floral scent enwrapped with woody warmth, luxurious with a touch of suede, but the possibilities are endless. It’s time to find your signature combination.

    What it includes:
    An iconic cream gift box tied with a ribbon.

    What else do you know:
    - Top Note: Red Apple. A crisp note that captures the mouth-watering juiciness of apple, opening the fragrance with an innocent, fruity twist.
    - Heart Note: Peony. An exquisite floral with delicate rose and honey accents and a subtle, green freshness. Fills the fragrance with the scent of peonies, just as they unfold.
    - Base Note: Suede. A supple textural note, complementing the floral opulence of peony and bringing enticing sensuality to the fragrance.

MORE INFORMATION ABOUT JO MALONE LONDON PEONY & BLUSH SUEDE COLOGNE

Alcohol Denat., Water, Fragrance, Benzyl Salicylate, Alpha-Isomethyl Ionone, Benzyl Alcohol, Geraniol, Hydroxycitronellal, Citronellol, Linalool, Eugenol, Limonene, Cinnamyl Alcohol, Isoeugenol, Benzyl Benzoate, Citral, Pentaerythrityl Tetra-Di-T-Butyl Hydroxyhydrocinnamate.

- Spritz generously onto your wrists, neck and pulse points.
- Wear alone or layer with another Cologne or your favourite Bath & Body products.
