    What it is:
    Sephora Collection’s cleansing makeup remover wipes pamper your skin while removing impurities. These effective face wipes provide a delightful sensory experience and are formulated with 99% naturally derived ingredients and plant extracts all infused in a base of plant-fiber fabric. This winning combo gently removes makeup, dirt, and impurities.

    What it does:
    To adapt to your skin’s needs, Sephora Collection’s cleansing makeup remover wipes have been formulated with carefully chosen plant extracts to deliver the full benefits of fruit and plants:

    - Coconut: Known for its high fatty acid content, coconut's hydrating properties are ideal for dry skin!
    - Watermelon: Bursting with water, watermelon is known for its high concentration of sugar and proteins, which help lock in moisture.
    - Aloe vera: Aloe vera is extremely resistant to heat. Its high sugar, protein, and amino acid content moisturizes skin and protects it from dehydration.

    What else you need to know:
    Say yes to quick cleansing! Our wipes for face and eyes deliver all the power of fruits and plants to rid your skin of impurities and remove makeup gently. Ideal for daily or occasional use (e.g., after exercising or when traveling). As part of Sephora Collection’s constant effort to minimize the use of plastics, these makeup remover wipes are made of plant fibers to gently cleanse your skin. 65% of the plastic in the packaging is made from sugarcane residue!
