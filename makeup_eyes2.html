Product Claims: Fragrance-free, Oil-free, Sulphate-free

- Storage Tip: Always store Tattoo Liner tip-down so the brush stays saturated.
- Shake before use to keep the formula flowing.
- Still feeling dry? Wipe tip with a damp tissue to remove eyeshadow build-up.
- When applying, use very light pressure for fine, exact lines (perfect for creating sharp cat eyeliner wings). Add more pressure to create a thicker, bold eyeliner look. The formula is also great for layering, so you can keep drawing over your lines until you achieve liner perfection.
- How to Fake Freckles: Using shade Mad Max Brown, make small dots wherever you want your faux freckles. Then use your fingers to tap the product into skin and make freckles appear more natural. For deeper skin tones, let the dots sit on skin for a few seconds before tapping.
