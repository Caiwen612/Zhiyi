  • Lips Makeup

    Lip makeup is the one makeup product we turn to for a way to make a statement, paint a megawatt smile or a sexy pout on our faces. Paint your lips and emphasise its natural shape in order to deliver your best smile, smirk or pout. With Sephora's complete range of makeup for the lips, you are guaranteed to achieve any look in the book! Given the range of lip makeup we offer, you are bound to find the exact combination of colour, finish and texture that you seek in a lippy.

    Lip Colour & Lipsticks

    Choose between the classic lipstick, the moisturising lip balm, or the glamorous lip gloss for a wide spectrum of colours that will satisfy even the most particular of tastes. Our all-inclusive collection of lip makeup also features a range of different finishes: shine, sheer, gloss, or matte, for your lips. Furthermore, these brands present a wide spectrum of colours, from bold and bright shades to nude and natural tones. Depending on your personal taste and preference, score through our online collection to pick out your favourite combination of colour, texture, formula and finish!

  • Lip Liner & Lip Brushes

    Going a step further than lip fillers, Sephora also offers lip liners and tools, used to define the natural shape of your lips. Lip liners are used to outline and define the shape of your lips and are typically handy to carry around and easy to use. For a more precise application of your lip makeup, we recommend using a lip brush which enables you to achieve better-defined lips. Lip brushes are used for a more-defined and accurate application of lip glosses and lipsticks as these brushes facilitate an even application of lip makeup and give a more acute definition of your lips.

    Lip Plumper & Lip Primer

    If you wish to go further than just dressing up your lips, opt for a lip plumper, which conditions and hydrates your lips to give you fuller and suppler lips. Our lip plumpers offer a nutrient-rich formula that promises to keep your lips plump, hydrated and lush. Hydrated and healthy lips enable a smoother application of your lip makeup, which makes this an awesome product to have! For enhancing our lip makeup, look towards a lip primer which minimizes the appearance of fine lines on our lips, thus priming them for a perfect coat of colour!
