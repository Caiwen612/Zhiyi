  • CIATÉ LONDON

    Plant Pots Nail Polish

    RM60.00

    DESCRIPTION

    Finish: Glitter, High Shine
    Formulation: Liquid
    Benefits: Strengthening

    What it is:
    A rich lacquer formulated with up to 80% natural ingredients, for an ultra-long wearing polish enriched with Ciaté’s exclusive nail care complex.

    What it does:
    This nail polish uses an exclusive complex to help treat nails. It is infused with some of nature’s most potent ingredients to rejuvenate (Bakuchiol), strengthen (Biotin) and repair (Bamboo Extract). A true ‘future thinking’ formulation; the polish contains patented technology that delivers a long-wearing, hardened and high-gloss shine, whilst Ciaté’s signature rounded edge brush makes for completely effortless, smooth application. Sustainability has also played a really vital part in the development of the Plant Pots; the bottles are made with recycled glass and have a recyclable cap, whilst all ingredients have been sustainably sourced, with all glitter shades being formulated with biodegradable glitters.
