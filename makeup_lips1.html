    Le Rouge Deep Velvet Lipstick

    RM145.00

    DESCRIPTION

    Finish: Matte
    Formulation: Cream, Stick
    Benefits: Long-wearing

    What it is:
    A new powdery matte and highly pigmented couture lipstick by Givenchy.

    What it does:
    This lipstick embodies the chic and sensorial attributes of the Givenchy maison. Covered in a sumptuous red velvet case echoing its powdery matte finish, it is the ultimate couture signature to your look. Comforting, soft and lightweight, the generous powdery texture glides effortlessly on the lip, delivering an ultra-matte colour from the very first stroke, with a super-thin film. Comfortable and long-wearing, the formula is infused with mango butter for absolute comfort and an intense makeup result. Le Rouge Deep Velvet comes in six shades, and covers the lips in an intense colour for up to 12 hours.
