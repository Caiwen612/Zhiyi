    Acqua Di Gio For Men Eau De Toilette

    RM372.00

    DESCRIPTION

    Fragrance Family: Citrus & Fruity, Earthy & Woody, Floral, Fresh
    Formulation: Liquid, Spray

    What it is:
    A hyacinth-infused men's fragrance that creates an aquatic splash of woody freshness.

    What it does:
    This floral, yet woody, hyacinth fragrance by Giorgio Armani opens with a splash of fresh, calabrian bergamot, neroli and green tangerine. Light, marine nuances intertwine with sweet jasmine fragrance, crisp rock rose, rosemary, fruity persimmon and warm Indonesian patchouli to create a men's fragrance that is both fresh and sensual.

Spray in a V-shape beginning at one shoulder and finishing at the other, to reveal your sensuality and boldness.
