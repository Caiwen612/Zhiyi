  • Foundation

    When it comes to foundations, there are a number of different types available on the market. In general, powder foundations work great for oily and combination skin types. Whereas, cream-based foundations complement those with normal to dry skin types who are looking for long-lasting, medium to full coverage in their daily foundation makeup. Browse through our phenomenal range online to find the best formula and type of foundation for your skin!

    Face Primer

    A primer is used for prepping your face, turning it into a smooth canvas and hiding the noticeable pores and blemishes for the next layer of foundation to be applied. Some primers can even be a standalone product that is finished with light setting powder, instead of another layer of foundation. Try an innovative primer product that smooths down the appearance of imperfections and blurs the lines, pores and creases on your face now.
